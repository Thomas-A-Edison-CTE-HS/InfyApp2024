Bulletin Board

We will focus on learning about how apps can be built to address a specific social issue (i.e. public health, climate change, food security, etc.).
• Goals – Students will determine two learning goals focused on the technology industry or knowledge to be developed through the completion of their app.
• Mentorship – Students will prepare a written agenda outlining progress, challenges, and questions related to their project and learning goals before each meeting.
• Research-based Work – A component requiring the use of online resources and conversations with their mentor and other colleagues to inform their final product.
• Weekly Milestones – All projects will have clear goals and deliverables for each week of the Infy App Challenge
• Peer Feedback – Each week the PENCIL team will facilitate cohort meetings for students to hear from each other and give/get feedback on the aspect of the app project they are responsible for. • Final Deliverable – Each student will create a working app to present within their issue.
